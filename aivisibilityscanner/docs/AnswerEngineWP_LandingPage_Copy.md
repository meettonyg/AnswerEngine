# SECTION 2: THE PROBLEM

**H2**

SEO gets you ranked. But AI doesn't rank — it extracts.

**Body**

ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews don't show ten blue links. They synthesize one answer and cite the sources they trust.

Your WordPress site was built for search engines that crawl HTML and reward backlinks. AI systems need structured knowledge — entity graphs, answer blocks, machine-readable feeds. Without them, your content doesn't exist to the fastest-growing discovery channel on the internet.

Most SEO plugins help you rank. They don't help you get quoted. AnswerEngineWP bridges the gap.

---

### Notes: The Problem

**Visual direction:**

- White background
- Two-column layout: text left (~60%), illustration right (~40%). Single column stacked on mobile.
- **Illustration — "The Shift":**
  - LEFT: Stylized Google SERP with 10 blue links. One highlighted at position #3. Label: "You rank here."
  - RIGHT: Stylized AI chat interface with a synthesized answer and a cited source URL. Label: "You get cited here."
  - Caption below both: "Which one drives more trust?"
- Flat/minimal style. Must read instantly at a glance.

**Copy rationale:**

- "AI doesn't rank — it extracts" is category-creation language. Draws a line between the old world and the new.
- "Cite the sources they trust" — "trust" implies citation is earned, not automatic.
- Three paragraphs: Problem → Implication → Bridge.
- "Your content doesn't exist" mirrors the "Invisible to AI" tier label from the scanner.
- "They help you rank. They don't help you get quoted." = one-line competitive positioning. No competitor names needed.
- No CTA in this section. Tension builds here; resolution comes next.

# SECTION 3: HOW IT WORKS

**H2**

From invisible to cited in 4 steps.

**Step 1 — Scan your site.**
Enter any URL and get your AI Visibility Score in under 10 seconds. Works on any website — yours, your clients', your competitors'.

**Step 2 — Install the free plugin.**
AnswerEngineWP runs alongside Yoast or Rank Math. No conflicts, no duplicate schema. Just the AI visibility layer your existing SEO plugin doesn't cover.

**Step 3 — Add Answer Blocks.**
Create structured content blocks AI systems prefer to cite — question, answer summary, and supporting facts — directly in the Gutenberg editor.

**Step 4 — Track your AI visibility over time.**
See which AI bots visit your site, what they can extract, and how your score improves as you optimize.

**Companion Mode Callout (highlighted card)**

> We don't replace your SEO plugin. We upgrade it for the AI era.
>
> AnswerEngineWP adds AI-specific structure — Speakable markup, Answer Blocks, and knowledge feeds — without duplicating the schema your SEO plugin already manages. Tested with Yoast SEO, Rank Math, and All in One SEO.

---

### Notes: How It Works

**Visual direction:**

- Light gray (`#F1F5F9`) background
- 4-step horizontal flow (desktop) / vertical stack (mobile)
- Each step: large numbered circle (1–4) in Primary Blue (`#2563EB`), icon, bold label, 1-sentence description
- Icons: magnifying glass (scan), download (install), content blocks (answer blocks), chart/trend line (track)
- Companion Mode callout: light blue (`#EFF6FF`) card with left blue (`#2563EB`) border. Show Yoast + Rank Math + AIOSEO logos inline.

**Copy rationale:**

- "From invisible to cited" compresses the entire product journey into five words, echoing the score tier language.
- Step 1 says "any website" — agencies need to audit prospects and clients, not just their own site.
- Step 4 reframed from "monitor AI crawlers" to "track your AI visibility over time" — outcome-tied, not mechanism-tied.
- Companion Mode callout uses "AI-specific structure" instead of "AEO-specific markup" — avoids internal jargon in visible copy.
- AIOSEO added to compatibility list — broadens addressable market at no cost.

# SECTION 5: AI CITATION SIMULATION

**H2**

See if AI would cite you — or your competitor.

**Body**

Enter your topic. See a simulated AI answer — and whether your site appears as a cited source. It's a structural stress-test: if your page has the right architecture, AI systems are more likely to extract and cite it.

**CTA**

Try it with your site →

**Disclaimer (small text)**

Simulated citation based on structural analysis — not a prediction of any specific AI system's behavior.

---

### Notes: Citation Simulation

**Visual direction:**

- Light blue (`#EFF6FF`) background — visually distinct from all other sections
- Centered mock AI chat interface:
  1. Prompt in "user message" bubble: *"Best CRM for real estate agents"*
  2. Synthesized answer in "AI response" bubble (3–4 sentences)
  3. "Sources cited" list with 3–4 URLs
  4. User's site: green + checkmark ("Cited") or red + X ("Not cited — here's why")
  5. Competitor URL in sources list if provided
- Clear "Simulated" label
- Design this to look good as a screenshot on Twitter/LinkedIn — most shareable visual on the page
- Consider 2–3 tabbed examples (CRM, travel, SaaS) so visitors see the concept without scanning their own site

**Copy rationale:**

- This section exists for screenshots. Agencies screenshot it and send it to clients.
- Body trimmed to two sentences. The visual does the work.
- Disclaimer is load-bearing. "Structural analysis" (verifiable) + "not a prediction" (clear boundary). Do not soften or remove.
- CTA is softer than hero — mid-page visitors are exploring, not ready for hard sell.
